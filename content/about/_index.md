#### The [Founder's](https://purpleteam-labs.com/about/) Unique Journey

Our foundation is built on a diverse career that blends practical building skills with high-tech analytical precision. Born and raised by the beach in Christchurch, New Zealand, the hands-on journey began early. Repairing surfboards since the age of nine and gaining school work experience at Canterbury Wetsuits (now called SeventhWave), which led to a full-time job at the same company. Then on to an apprenticeship in carpentry, followed by various carpentry foreman roles, a carpentry business, providing a strong foundation in practical construction and durable building techniques.

This was followed by 23 years in technology and consultancy, navigating over 35 roles. Beginning as a Software Tester, this career evolved through roles like Software Engineer, Software Architect, and specialised security roles, including penetration testing and Application Security Engineer.
